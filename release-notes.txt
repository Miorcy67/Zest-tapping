Zest Tapping Forge 1.8.9

Features:
- Practice overlay for hold/release/strafe rhythm
- Low, medium, and high ping training modes
- Command support via /zesttap
- Clean HUD panel with trainer guidance

How to use:
1. Place the mod jar in your Minecraft 1.8.9 Forge mods folder.
2. Launch Minecraft and press P to toggle the overlay.
3. Use /zesttap mode <low|medium|high> to change the timing profile.
